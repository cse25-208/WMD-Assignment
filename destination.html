The Okavango Delta

A living peaceful haven in the heart of the Kalahari.

The Pride of Botswana

Also described as the "Jewel of the Kalahari", the Okavango Delta is a unique inland delta that transforms into a lush oasis during the rainy season. It is home to an incredible diversity of wildlife, including elephants, hippos, crocodiles, and a myriad of bird species.- set in the harsh and arid surroundings of Botswana's bush and desert, is widely regarded as one of Africa's premier wildlife destinations. The delta's intricate network of waterways, lagoons, and islands creates a haven for both animals and nature enthusiasts alike, - offering unforgettable safari experiences and a chance to witness the raw beauty of the African wilderness.
