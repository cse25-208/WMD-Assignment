Wildlife of Botswana

Botswana is renowned for its rich biodiversity and abundant wildlife. The country is home to a wide variety of animals, including elephants, lions, leopards, cheetahs, giraffes, zebras, and many more. The Okavango Delta is a particularly important habitat for wildlife, providing a haven for numerous species. Visitors to Botswana can experience incredible wildlife sightings through camps, safaris explore safaris and guided tours in national parks and reserves.
